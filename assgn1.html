Task

The main task of this assignment is visualising our ideas based on the 3 prompts that we chose.
Our ideas will then have to be eloborated through the ideation process using the concept of deuctive reasoning and questioning.
Our ideas have no boundaries set and we're able to imagine the most bizzare things we can do.
