Toy Design : Play and The Act of Play

Task

Design and create a toy using recycled or items that can be found in our homes for a specific body part.

I was assigned to make a toy that is related to legs and feet.

Outcome

Marble Monster

A toy that requires the player to stand on top of the tins and balancing the marbles between the 2 bottles.
This toy is also modular, it is able to connect to multiple bottles allowing more players to play together.

Reflection

This assignment required a lot of teamwork as there are many aspects that we had to explore and research on. Our group members used WhatsApp to communicate and had no trouble communicating with each other. Although we had different ideas in the beginning, we managed to accept and combine to come up with an idea that we all loved. This assignment has taught me to be tolerable and always listen to other opinions in a group project in order to work smoothly.
