Taylor's Graduate Capabilities

Most of the TGC's have been applied throughout my experience in this module. Some of the notable TGC's are Thinking And Problem Solving Skills, Communication Skills, Interpersonal Skills and Intrapersonal Skills. All of the assignments required a lot of of creative thinking and analytical thinking to solve puzzles and come up with new ideas. Digital Literacy also played a big role for me throughout this module, as observed, this e-portfolio was completely built manually using HTML, CSS and JavaScript programming languages and has greatly improved my web development skills and programming skills.
